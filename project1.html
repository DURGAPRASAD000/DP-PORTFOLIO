Conversational QA Chatbot

This project showcases a cutting-edge Conversational QA Chatbot built using RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) architecture. Leveraging sentence-transformers and vector search, it integrates LLMs such as GPT, LLaMA, and Falcon with intelligent prompt engineering to maintain policy compliance, semantic search accuracy, and conversational context.

The bot was deployed with a streamlined backend, capable of handling user queries in real-time using fine-tuned embeddings and prompt-chaining strategies. The design is modular, scalable, and easy to integrate into enterprise systems for smart customer support and knowledge discovery.
